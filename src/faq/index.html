Frequently Asked Questions:

  • Q: This seems like a pretty cool game, where can I look into it?
  • A: You can go back to the main page and click the overview image to be take directly to the Steam Page for this game.

  • Q: How many monsters are there in this game?
  • A: There are currently 31 large monsters and 17 small monsters

  • Q: Are there different weapons in this game?
  • A: Yes! Each weapon plays quite differently. If you plan to master each weapon, it is like playing a whole new game.

  • A: Currently there are 14 different weapons, offering 14 different feeling ways to play the game.
